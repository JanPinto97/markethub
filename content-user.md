# MarketHub — What You Can Do and What You Get

MarketHub combines a real-time financial dashboard with a finance-focused social network. This document focuses on **what a user can actually do** on the platform and **what they get in return**.

---

## What MarketHub gives you

- **A single place to follow the markets** — live prices, charts and macro events without juggling multiple sites.
- **A community of investors and traders** — share ideas, ask questions, and learn from other people's analyses.
- **Context, not just numbers** — every chart sits next to news, sentiment and a calendar of upcoming events that move the markets.
- **A personal financial home** — your watchlist, your communities, your followers, your saved topics, all in one account.

---

## Things you can do without signing in

You can explore most of the platform as a guest before committing to an account:

- Browse the **Community feed** and read what people are saying about the markets.
- Visit any **public community** and read its posts.
- Open any **discussion topic** (e.g. Macro, Crypto, Trading) and read its threads.
- Read a **post in full** with all its comments and replies.
- Visit any **user's public profile**, see their bio, their public posts and the communities they belong to.
- Use the **global search** to find users, posts or communities.
- Open the **Markets dashboard** and watch live prices, charts, news and the economic calendar.
- Check the **global market session status** (Tokyo / London / New York) in the header at any time.

Anything that involves *creating* or *interacting* (posting, voting, joining, following, commenting, replying) requires a free account.

## What you can do once you sign in

### Manage your account

- **Create an account** with username, email and password, or **sign in** to an existing one.
- Edit your **profile** at any time: avatar, cover image, display username, bio.
- Change your **email** and **password** from a single settings page.
- Log out from anywhere via the avatar dropdown in the header.

### Build your social presence

- Set up a **public profile page** that anyone can visit at `/profile/<username>`.
- **Follow** other users and build a personal "Following" feed.
- See **who follows you** and **who you follow** in a dedicated modal.
- Share posts that appear on your profile timeline.

### Post and interact in the main feed

- Publish a **short post** (up to 400 characters) with optional **image or video** attached.
- Decorate posts with **emojis** from a built-in picker.
- **Like** any post (with an instant visual response).
- **Comment** on any post and read other users' comments.
- **Open a private chat-style discussion** from any comment, so a thread can keep going without flooding the main feed.
- **Switch between "Trending" and "Following"** to focus either on what's hot or only on people you follow.
- **Edit or delete** your own posts. Moderators and admins can also remove others' posts.
- **Report** a post that breaks the rules.

### Join and run communities

MarketHub offers two flavours of communities:

- **Public communities** — anyone can read and join with a single click.
- **Private communities** — joining requires sending a request with an optional message.

What you can do in any community you've joined:

- **Post** to the community (same rich create-post box as in the main feed).
- **Read the community feed**, with pinned posts at the top.
- **Leave** the community any time. Leaving the last member auto-deletes it (you'll be warned).

What you can do as a **community leader**:

- **Create** a new public or private community from scratch (name, description, avatar, type).
- **Approve or reject** pending join requests (with the requester's message in front of you).
- **Expel** members.
- **Promote** members to Moderator, Whale, etc.
- **Pin** posts so they always show first.
- **Delete** the community entirely (with explicit confirmation).

What you can do as a **moderator**: accept/reject join requests, delete inappropriate posts and comments.

### Discover new communities and topics

- Click **Discover Communities** to open a popup with every community on the platform.
  - **Search** by name (debounced).
  - **Sort** by popularity, member count, or newest.
  - **Filter** by type (public / private).
  - **Join** with one click — newly joined communities show up immediately in your sidebar.
- Click **Add Topics** to **pin** any discussion topic to your sidebar so it's one click away.
  - Filter topics by category: Core Markets, Macro, Assets, Trading.

### Take part in topic discussions

Topics work like a Reddit-style forum focused on a specific theme.

- **Read** the topic's posts sorted by **Top** or **Recent**.
- **Create a new post** in the topic with a title (required, up to 300 chars), optional text body (up to 2000 chars) and media.
- **Upvote or downvote** any post.
- **Open a post in full** to see the full body, media and discussion.
- **Comment** on any post.
- **Reply** to any comment (one level of nesting — replies always attach to the root comment).
- **Delete** your own posts and comments; moderators can also remove others'.

### Have private conversations

- From any post comment in the main feed, **spin off a private discussion** with the comment's author.
- The discussion uses a **chat-style UI** with auto-resizing input and message history.
- You can **reference a specific message** by replying to it (it shows up as a quoted preview).

### Search across the whole platform

- Use the search bar in the header for a **fast dropdown** of top results across Users, Posts and Communities (keyboard-navigable: ↑ ↓ Enter Esc).
- Or open the **full search page** for tabs, pagination and 10 results per page.

## What you can do and see in the Markets dashboard

The Markets section is your real-time financial cockpit.

### Track live prices

- View a **watchlist of live tickers** spanning the most-watched markets:
  - **Crypto**: BTC, ETH and many more.
  - **Forex**: EUR/USD and other major pairs.
  - **Commodities**: gold (XAU), crude oil (WTI).
  - **Indices**: S&P 500, NASDAQ 100.
  - **Stocks**: AAPL, NVDA, TSLA and others.
- Each row shows the **current price**, the **percent change** for the day, and a quick visual trend.
- Click any row to **load it into the main interactive chart**.

### Search any symbol

- Open the **search dropdown** and start typing — results stream in across multiple data providers (Finnhub, Twelve Data, CoinGecko).
- Use the keyboard (↑ ↓ Enter Esc) to navigate the results.
- Pick a result to **switch the dashboard to that asset**.

### Read interactive charts

- A full **TradingView widget** powers the main chart:
  - Pick a **timeframe** (from minutes to monthly).
  - Add **technical indicators** (moving averages, RSI, MACD, volume, etc.).
  - Use **drawing tools** to mark trendlines, support/resistance, Fibonacci levels.
- Switch to the **Charts tab** for a dedicated playground with multiple symbol overlays and saved annotations.

### Stay on top of macro events

- The **Economic Calendar** tab shows a date-filtered table of upcoming and past events:
  - **Impact** (low / medium / high) so you can spot the market movers.
  - **Country**, event name, **forecast** and **actual** values, and the scheduled time.
- Jump to a specific date with the date picker.
- Filter by impact or country to cut down the noise.

### Read finance news

- The **News tab** aggregates headlines from several providers (NewsData, Marketaux, etc.).
- Each article shows headline, source, timestamp, snippet and image.
- Click to read the **full preview** in-app, with a link to the original source.
- A teaser block on the **Overview tab** lets you jump straight to a specific article.

### Read the room

- The **Market Sentiment** indicator (Fear & Greed style) tells you the overall mood at a glance — from "Extreme Fear" to "Extreme Greed".

### Always know if the market is open

- The header carries **three real-time badges** for Tokyo, London and New York:
  - Green when the session is **open**, with a countdown to close on hover.
  - Amber and pulsing during the **30-minute pre-open**.
  - Grey when **closed**, with a countdown to the next open.
- The trading window is displayed in **UTC** so you don't have to convert timezones in your head.

## What this means for you in practice

- **For a curious beginner** — read posts in public communities, follow experienced users, and use the topics to learn how people think about specific assets, all while watching the actual prices move next door.
- **For an active trader** — keep your watchlist on one tab, the calendar on another, and use the community to crowd-check your reading of the news.
- **For a community builder** — spin up a public or private community around your niche (a specific stock, a strategy, a region), gate it with join requests if you want, and curate the conversation through pins and moderation.
- **For anyone** — get one consistent place where the *signal* (prices, calendar, news) and the *conversation* (posts, topics, discussions) live side by side, instead of having to bounce between five different apps.
